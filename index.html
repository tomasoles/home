Welcome! I am an assistant professor at the University of Economics in Bratislava. I am particularly interested in understanding of the implications of automation and new technologies on the labor market. My previous research mainly concentrated on the empirical analysis of business cycle synchronization and the dynamics of economic convergence within the EU.

Download my CV or contact me on my email.
